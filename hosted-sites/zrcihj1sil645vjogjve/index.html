Our Story, Our Legacy

Nestled on Santa Monica Boulevard, Dan Tana's has been a beloved West Hollywood institution since 1964. We pride ourselves on serving timeless Italian classics in an atmosphere that's as vibrant and authentic as our dishes. From the moment you step through our doors, you become part of our extended family.

We are immensely grateful for the overwhelming demand for our indoor and outdoor dining experiences. Your patronage and loyalty mean the world to us, especially as we continue to evolve. Every dish is crafted with passion, every cocktail poured with precision, ensuring an unforgettable experience for our cherished guests.

Forever grateful,
The Dan Tana's Team
