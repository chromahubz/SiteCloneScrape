Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind SiteClone Pro with a comprehensive refund policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer full refunds within the specified timeframe for your purchase type.

Refund Policy Overview

Our refund policy varies depending on how you purchased SiteClone Pro. Please review the section that applies to your purchase.

1. AppSumo Purchases

60-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All purchases made through AppSumo are covered by AppSumo's 60-day money-back guarantee.

Timeframe

You have 60 days from the date of purchase to request a full refund.

How to Request

Log into your AppSumo account and request a refund through their platform. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

No Questions Asked

AppSumo offers hassle-free refunds. You don't need to provide a reason, though we appreciate feedback!

2. Direct Purchases (Monthly/Annual)

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Purchases made directly through our website come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

Timeframe

Request a refund within 30 days of your initial purchase or renewal date.

How to Request

Email support@siteclonepro.com with your account email and request a refund. We'll process it within 5-7 business days.

Refund Amount

You'll receive a full refund of your subscription fee. Processing fees are non-refundable.

3. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

Eligible

  • - Request made within refund period
  • - First-time purchase (no prior refunds)
  • - Normal usage of the service
  • - Valid account in good standing

Not Eligible

  • - Request after refund period expires
  • - Previous refund received
  • - Terms of Service violations
  • - Excessive or abusive usage

4. API Costs and Third-Party Services

Important Notice

SiteClone Pro refunds only cover our subscription fees. We cannot refund costs incurred through third-party services:

  • - OpenAI API usage charges
  • - Anthropic (Claude) API charges
  • - Google (Gemini) API charges
  • - Firecrawl or other scraping service costs

These charges are billed directly by the respective service providers and are subject to their refund policies.

7. Subscription Cancellations

Cancel Anytime

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Here's what happens:

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund if I've used the service extensively?

Yes, as long as you're within the refund period. Normal usage doesn't disqualify you. However, excessive or abusive usage (e.g., scraping thousands of sites) may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

What happens to my data after a refund?

Your data is retained for 30 days after refund in case you change your mind. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted from our servers.

Can I get a refund for my annual subscription mid-year?

The 30-day refund window applies from your purchase or renewal date. If you cancel an annual subscription outside the refund window, it will remain active until the end of the year, but you won't be charged for the following year.

Do you offer refunds for technical issues?

We'll work with you to resolve any technical issues before considering a refund. If we can't resolve the issue to your satisfaction within the refund period, you're eligible for a full refund.
