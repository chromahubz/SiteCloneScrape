General Questions

What is SiteClone Pro?

SiteClone Pro is an all-in-one tool that scrapes websites, extracts business data, and uses AI to recreate them as modern, responsive designs. It's perfect for web designers, agencies, and freelancers looking to generate leads and create website redesign proposals quickly.

Who is SiteClone Pro for?

SiteClone Pro is designed for web designers, web development agencies, freelancers, digital marketers, and sales teams who want to identify outdated websites, create modern redesign proposals, and generate personalized outreach emails to win new clients.

Do I need coding knowledge to use SiteClone Pro?

No coding knowledge is required! SiteClone Pro is designed to be user-friendly for anyone. Simply enter a URL, click a button, and let the AI do the work. However, basic HTML/CSS knowledge can be helpful if you want to customize the generated designs.

Is SiteClone Pro legal to use?

Yes! SiteClone Pro extracts publicly available information from websites, similar to how search engines work. However, you should always respect copyright and intellectual property laws. Use the tool to create proposals and redesigns for potential clients, not to copy and republish someone else's content.

Scraping Questions

How many pages can I scrape at once?

The default limit is 100 pages per scrape session. This is sufficient for most small to medium-sized business websites. If you need to scrape larger sites, contact our support team for custom limits.

How long does scraping take?

Most websites are scraped in 1-3 minutes, depending on the size and complexity. Larger sites with more pages may take 5-10 minutes. You'll see a progress indicator during the scraping process.

What if a website can't be scraped?

Some websites have protections that prevent automated scraping. If scraping fails, try accessing the site directly to ensure it's online. JavaScript-heavy sites or those requiring login may have limited data extraction. Contact support if you're having consistent issues.

Can I scrape websites that require login?

No, SiteClone Pro can only scrape publicly accessible content. Content behind login walls, paywalls, or authentication cannot be accessed.

AI Recreation Questions

Which AI model should I use?

It depends on your needs. GPT-4 offers the most creative and complex designs but is slower and more expensive. Claude provides professional, clean designs with great code quality. Gemini is the fastest and most cost-effective option. We recommend trying different models to see which works best for your projects.

Do I need my own API keys?

Yes, you'll need to provide your own API keys for the AI models you want to use. This gives you full control over usage and costs. We provide step-by-step guides for obtaining keys from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google.

How much do API calls cost?

Costs vary by model. Gemini typically costs $0.10-0.50 per website recreation, Claude around $0.50-1.50, and GPT-4 between $1.00-3.00 depending on website complexity. You pay the AI providers directly, and SiteClone Pro doesn't take a cut.

Can I customize the generated designs?

Absolutely! You have full access to the HTML/CSS code and can edit it however you like. You can also regenerate designs with different prompts or color schemes to get variations.

Outreach Questions

How does the outreach generator work?

After scraping a website, our AI analyzes the business information and generates personalized cold emails. These emails reference specific details from the target website to make your outreach more relevant and effective.

Can I edit the generated emails?

Yes! All generated emails are fully editable. We recommend reviewing and customizing each email to add your personal touch before sending. Think of them as templates that give you a strong starting point.

Does SiteClone Pro send emails for me?

No, SiteClone Pro generates email templates for you to copy and send using your own email client or outreach tool. This gives you full control over your outreach campaigns and ensures deliverability.
